Alternative Therapy

Discover holistic and natural approaches to healing through our alternative therapy services. Designed to restore balance and promote well-being.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a time-tested alternative therapy that uses thin, sterile needles inserted at specific points in the body to stimulate energy flow, promote healing, and restore balance.

Cupping (Dry/Wet/Fire etc)

Cupping Therapy is a traditional alternative treatment that involves placing cups on the skin to create suction. This technique enhances circulation, promotes healing, and relieves pain.

Sujok

Sujok Therapy is a healing technique that uses the hands and feet as mirrors of the body. It involves stimulating specific points on the palms, fingers, and soles of the feet to promote healing, relieve pain, and balance energy.

Auricular

Auricular Therapy is an alternative healing technique that involves stimulating specific points on the ear to promote overall health and well-being. Based on the principles of Traditional Chinese Medicine, the ear is seen as a microsystem that reflects the entire body.

Soft Laser Acupunture

Unlike traditional acupuncture, which uses needles, this technique employs light to promote healing, relieve pain, and restore energy balance without discomfort or invasiveness. It uses low-level lasers (soft laser) to stimulate acupuncture points in the body.
